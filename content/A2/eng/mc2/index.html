Ready for offline use

We want to work together to build multiplying churches and faith communities so that there is one church for every 1,000 people across Australia.


1.
Your Kingdom Come
MC2 is designed to help your team follow the model that Jesus used for establishing multiplying spiritual communities so that God's kingdom expands throughout the earth.
2.
The Model Jesus Gave Us
In this session, you will discover an overview of how Jesus developed a movement of disciple-making by using multiplying leaders to carry his kingdom vision for all nations.
3.
The MyFriends Lifestyle
The MyFriends Lifestyle  helps you and your non-Christian friends build stronger bonds with each other around God's story.
4.
Phase 2: Ministry Foundations
The Ministry Foundations Phase is a time for leaders to build good relationships in preparation for the expanded ministry ahead. It is a time when we use our resources and energy to build an atmosphere of love, trust and acceptance as a foundation for future ministry.
5.
Holy Spirit Dependence
The Scriptures are clear — every aspect of Jesus’ life and ministry was saturated with the Spirit of God. How could you train your people in a transferable way about the role of the Holy Spirit?
6.
Prayer
Prayer saturated every aspect of Christ’s life and ministry. His ministry began in prayer and ended in prayer. How might you create a culture of prayer in your people?
7.
Obedience to a Kingdom Agenda
Call people to understand their context and culture, and to partner with God’s Kingdom agenda in their country.
8.
Ministry of the Word
God’s Word was central in Christ’s life and ministry. Jesus knew the Word and used it as He encountered the everyday issues of life. How might you create a culture of the love and application of the Word in your people?
9.
Exhalting the Father
One of Christ’s unique priorities was to continually exalt His Father in every area of life. How can you train your people to exalt the Lord as a way of life, not just an event once a week.
10.
Relationships that are intentional
Jesus modelled intentional relationships throughout His life. How can you bring Jesus into our connection with others?
