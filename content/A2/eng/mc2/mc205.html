5.

Holy Spirit Dependence

The Scriptures are clear — every aspect of Jesus’ life and ministry was saturated with the Spirit of God.

Jesus was:

  • Conceived by the Spirit ( Luke 1:35 )
  • Anointed by the Spirit ( Luke 4:18 , Acts 10:38 , Isaiah 11:1–2 )
  • Filled with the Spirit (Luke 4:1, 14; John 3:34)
  • Sealed by the Spirit (John 6:27)
  • Led by the Spirit (Luke 4:1)
  • Rejoiced in the Spirit (Luke 10:21)
  • Gave commands by the Spirit (Acts 1:2)
  • Performed miracles by the power of the Spirit (Matthew 12:28, Luke 4:14–15, 18)
  • Was raised by the Spirit (Hebrews 9:14; Romans 8:11)

In the MyFriends Lifestyle we introduce people to the role of the Holy Spirit through an article called Satisfied.

Applying This Priority to Your Ministry

  • Live like the Holy Spirit is your BEST FRIEND (John 14:15-16, Acts 15:28).
  • What would demonstrate that you depend upon the Holy Spirit for guidance into new ministry opportunities (Acts 1:8; 10:45; 13:2)?
  • How might you communicate the ongoing role of the Holy Spirit in a believer’s life (John 14:25, 1 Corinthians 2:9–10)?
  • How might you create a culture of Holy Spirit dependence in your people?
