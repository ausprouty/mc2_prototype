Obedience to a Kingdom Agenda

Over 70 verses in the New Testament speak of the “Kingdom of God,” and another 30 reference the “Kingdom of Heaven.”

“Your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.” (Matt 6:10)

+ A Kingdom culture is fuelled by:

  1. Clear compelling vision of a preferred future.
  2. A message of hope.
  3. A lifestyle of grace and truth.
  4. An attitude of expectancy.
  5. A heart of obedience.

Kingdom culture

Is fueled by: Following the Holy Spirit as we practice the way of Jesus in love, prayer & disciple making, together.


The culture of heaven that we seek to cultivate on earth-

    Prayerful dependence on the Holy Spirit (Jesus often withdrew to pray)
    Obeying a kingdom agenda not the world, flesh or devils agenda. (Does it  point to Jesus? John Baptist example  in John 3.20-30)
    Word of God is central (Luke 3- man does not live by bread)
    Exalting the Father (It’s not about me! Jesus always pointed up to Father John 5.19)
    Relationally centered. Jesus was more interested in people than programs. He did life on life (incarnational) Ministry.


= People become like him and with His presence (Holy Spirit) and Power (His Authority as King imparted to us as ambassadors) bring the influence of heaven into every place of societal influence.

We embody the good-news of grace, truth, freedom, forgiveness, healing, restoration and eternal hope in Jesus and establish those kingdom realities in communities on earth as it is in heaven. (Lords Prayer)


    The kingdom within us is meant to influence the world around us.


"Leaders are to rule with the heart of a servant, and serve with the heart of a king"- Bill Johnson


'The scriptures describe men and women of God who cultivated these qualities (godly attributes developed in the secret places) and took hold of their positions of divine influence to shift the destinies of nations.

Their prominence was not for their own benefit, but for the purpose of shaping society toward God's original intent.

They created catalytic cultures that maximized the godly influence of their constituents on the rest of creation...'

Destined to win, Captain America: K.V

Other thoughts:
1. We live from grace and with truth.
2. We embrace humility and honour.
3. We are always with Jesus.
4. We are dependent on His Spirit.
5. We live with Gods heart for us, our family and many far off to know the love & life of Jesus.
