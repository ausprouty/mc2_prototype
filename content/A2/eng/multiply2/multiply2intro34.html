Period 3 and Period 4

Period 3: Ministry Training

Period 4: Expanded Outreach:

around Late 27 AD – Late 28 AD

Recruit, train and mobilize a growing mission team into broad sowing and making new disciples


Period 3 Ministry Training
This period lasts for 6-9 months and starts with Jesus moving to Capernaum. During this period, Jesus challenges the four fishermen (Mark 1:16-20) who had already been followers of Jesus since His baptism. Jesus begins to change their priorities and they become trainees in fishing for men. These four men form the initial mission team but Jesus soon adds Levi (Mark 2:13-17) and others.


Period 4 Expanded Outreach
This period lasts for 6-9 months and runs parallel with Period 3. While Jesus is recruiting an initial ministry team to share in the work of the mission, He generates opportunities for their ‘field training’ through local (Mark 1:21-34) outreaches, regional outreaches and expanded campaigns (Mark 1:35-39). This expanded outreach is characterized by an acceleration of the movement but also brings increased opposition (Mark 3:6).
