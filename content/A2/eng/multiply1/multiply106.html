Motivation and Encouragement

God has given us every spiritual blessing through Jesus. He has adopted us into His family and calls us His children. “You were included in Christ when you heard the word of truth, the gospel of your salvation. Having believed, you were marked in Him with a seal, the promised Holy Spirit, who is a deposit guaranteeing our inheritance.” (Ephesians 3:13-14a) What an amazing gift! This is God’s promise for you.

Context

After Jesus’ resurrection, He appeared to His disciples many times during a 40-day period. One of the times He appeared while they were meeting in a large room and He explained the importance of His Word.

Read or watch Luke 24:36-49 two times as everyone listens

37 They were startled and frightened, thinking they saw a ghost.38 He said to them, “Why are you troubled, and why do doubts rise in your minds?39 Look at my hands and my feet. It is I myself! Touch me and see; a ghost does not have flesh and bones, as you see I have.”

+ Summary

Jesus wants us to understand God’s Word and what it teaches us about Him. Jesus’ life fulfilled the promises in God’s Word. He wants us to tell others about what we learn. 

Read 2 Timothy 3:16-17 , Romans 10:17 and Hebrews 4:12 to highlight truths about God’s Word. 

Reading the Bible each day is an important part of getting to know Jesus. 

LOOK FORWARD

+ Preparing for Mission

Make a commitment to read at least one chapter from the Bible each day, beginning with the Gospel of John. 

Pray that the Holy Spirit will teach you through His Word, read the chapter, and then answer five questions:

  1. What stands out for you in this passage?
  2. What do you like and what bothers you in this passage?
  3. What does this passage tell you about God and people?
  4. Ask God what He wants you to do in response to the passage. Is there:
  • A behaviour to change?
  • A promise to claim?
  • An example to follow?
  • A command to obey?
  1. Who can I help with this passage?
