+ Summary

As the movement grew, Jesus had a hard time finding a place to rest undisturbed. He was so tired that he slept through a storm on the lake! Yes, Jesus got tired just like we do. He was fully human and became like us, His own creations ( Hebrews 2:17 ). Jesus started to use the Lake of Galilee as a place to escape and rest with His leaders. A strong wind and furious storm brought water into the boat. The new leaders were struck with fear and cried out to Jesus for help. He rebuked them for not having faith. He expected them to trust that He would not let harm come to them.
