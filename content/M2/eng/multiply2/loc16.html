Summary

Jesus now moves into a period of transition in His ministry. At the beginning of this period, John was put in prison and this prompted Jesus to move to north to Galilee. Jesus was then rejected in his home village of Nazareth. He relocates, choosing the city of Capernaum on the lake shore as his new home base. This is a strategic location as it is on a major international highway of that time. Jesus’ fame is growing and spreading. With John the Baptist now in prison, there is a transition of leadership of the Kingdom message. Jesus begins to focus His message to the same message that John had, “repent for the kingdom of heaven is near.”
