Summary

Read Luke 3:23. Jesus moved south to an area near Jericho, about 3-4 days journey from his home in Galilee. He may not have been sure of His next steps, but the Father led Him to be baptized by John. Baptism was used to identify a messenger and his message. Luke 3:23 says he is about 30 years old when he began his ministry.

Further Study: Luke 3:21-23; Mark 1: 9-11
