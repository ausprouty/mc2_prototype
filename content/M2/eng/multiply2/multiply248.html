+ Summary

Jesus has spent the last couple of years teaching people about living in the Kingdom of God and what the Kingdom of God is like. During this last evening together, Jesus gives additional teaching about His departure and the gift of the Spirit that will be given to help His followers live out the ways of the Kingdom. His followers are confused about what is about to happen in the hours ahead, but Jesus clarifies that He is God in the flesh. Jesus is our perfect model for life and ministry. We understand God because He is revealed through the life of Jesus. Jesus did His works through the Father, and we will do the same work of disciple-making as Jesus did by the power of His Spirit. Jesus also models leadership that submits to the Father and does His will through His power. He is reminding them of the foundations of Holy Spirit dependence: prayer; obedience to the Father’s Kingdom agenda; the priority of the Word of God, including Jesus’ own words; exalting the Father; and intentional relationships of love and sacrifice.
