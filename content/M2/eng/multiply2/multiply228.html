+ Summary

Jesus explains more about kingdom living to the disciples. Part of the plan for Kingdom expansion is to love others, even your enemies. It is a love that expects nothing in return. It is a radical, God-like love that will change the world. Part of loving others includes no longer judging other people and no longer condemning other people for their actions. Instead, extend forgiveness in Jesus’ name, not because they deserve it but because Jesus instructs us to forgive. We should take some time to examine our own lives. He instructs the leaders that “the blind cannot lead the blind.” Jesus expects the disciples to model this lifestyle. He reminds them that they need to watch His life and ministry closely. “Don’t call me Lord if you are not going to do what I say.”
